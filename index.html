  • OUR MISSION
    To develop an AI based low-cost and portable device for onsite waste segregation.
    The device is a blend of hardware & software.The hardware is connected to a CNN based machine learning model that classifies the waste.
    ML model and hardware is connected to a user friendly website. The output of the ML model is passed to the user-friendly website.
    The live details of amount of waste in each category is displayed on the website and the concerned industries are notified.
    For eg: if the amount of paper segregated reaches beyond a threshold then paper recycling or reusing industries are notified.
    Our Goals
    Our goal is to manage 500+ tonnes of waste per day by 2026.
    We ensure maximum recovery of resources from all streams of waste by diverting it away from landfills and sending it to authorised destinations.
